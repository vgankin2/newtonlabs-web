Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Cadence is built on a simple principle: your health data belongs to you. We do not collect it, sell it, or share it with anyone.

What data Cadence accesses

Cadence reads step count, walking and running distance, and flights climbed from Apple Health on your device. This data is used solely to display your activity within the app.

Cadence never writes to Apple Health and never modifies your Health data in any way.

Where your data lives

All health data and activity history is stored in two places:

  • On your device — in the app's private document storage, accessible only to Cadence.
  • In your private iCloud — if you are signed into iCloud, Cadence uses Apple's CloudKit to sync your history and settings across your own devices. This data is stored in your personal iCloud account and is never accessible to Newton Labs or any third party.

Data we do not collect

Newton Labs does not operate any servers, does not collect analytics, and does not receive any personal data from Cadence. Specifically:

  • No names, email addresses, or account information
  • No location data
  • No health or fitness data
  • No usage analytics or crash reporting sent to us
  • No advertising identifiers

Tip jar purchases

Cadence offers an optional tip jar through Apple's in-app purchase system (StoreKit). All payment processing is handled entirely by Apple. Newton Labs never receives, stores, or processes any payment information or personal details associated with a purchase.

Apple Health permissions

Cadence requests read-only access to Steps, Walking + Running Distance, and Flights Climbed in Apple Health. You can review and revoke these permissions at any time in:

Settings → Privacy & Security → Health → Cadence

Children's privacy

Cadence does not knowingly collect any data from children under the age of 13. As noted above, the app does not collect personal data from any user regardless of age.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this privacy policy, we will update the date at the top of this page.

Contact
